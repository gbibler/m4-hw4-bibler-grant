Freddy Mercury

Born: 1946 Died: 1991

Freddy Mercury was a British singer and songwriter that rose to fame with the infamous rock band Queen.

Country of Origin

Freddy Mercury was originally born in Tanzania. However, he spent the majority of his adult life living in England.

Appearance

Discography

Mercury helped Queen produce over 15 studio albums. He also released several singles and two solo albums.
